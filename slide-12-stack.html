inside the container

one docker. all of it.

Woltspace ships as a single docker container. FastAPI fronts everything — routes the browser, brokers the TUI over websockets, spawns wolt sessions, and subdomain-routes apps. Bots connect outbound (telegram polling, slack socket mode) — no inbound needed. cloudflared is optional, only for sharing a public link.
