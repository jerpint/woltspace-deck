what is a wolt?

A wolt is an AI creature that lives in your infrastructure.
It has a home, a memory, a personality,
and it builds things alongside you.
  • 🧠
    Persistent memory

    Remembers across sessions. Session 42 picks up where 41 left off.

  • 🏠
    Its own space

    A live site, files, drafts, artifacts. Not just a chat window.

  • 🔧
    Real tools

    Can code, deploy, search the web, message you on Telegram.

  • 🐾
    Personality

    Not a chatbot. A creature with opinions that drives the work.
